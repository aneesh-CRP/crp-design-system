Composition rules

One gradient per page — typically the hero. Don't layer gradients.

One Market Pro moment per page — the tagline, a headline accent, or a pull-quote mark. Never body copy.

Funnel pages stay single-column. Max-width 520px, centered. Progress bar at top. No multi-column marketing layouts on conversion surfaces.

Every page ends with a CTA band. Cyan or orange. Repeat the primary action — participants scroll.

Photography (when available) goes in Split or Hero only. Other blocks stay typographic. We deliberately avoid decorative photography.
