Template rules

Keep every staff signature visually identical.

  • Logo: crp-stacked.svg (or PNG) at 120px wide, left-aligned, vertically centered with text.
  • Divider: 1px solid #d0d0d0 vertical rule, 28px padding on each side.
  • Font: Arial / Helvetica, sans-serif — never embed Area Normal (email clients strip @font-face).
  • Text color: #333 name, #555 body, #1155cc links (Gmail default blue).
  • Phone icon: green handset #0a8a3a between phone number and website — inline SVG, 14px.
  • Labels (Phone:, Fax:, Email:) left-aligned in a 46px column.
  • Two addresses: both linked to Google Maps, stacked, blue + underline.
  • Socials: 4 icons (FB, IG, LinkedIn, TikTok) in grey #555, 20px, 12px gap.
  • No tagline, no quote, no "think before you print," no department lines.
