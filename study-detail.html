Who this is for

You might be a good fit for CRP-247 if…

Eligibility is confirmed at your first visit with a free screening. The three-minute questionnaire below is a starting point, not a diagnosis.

✓Likely to qualify

  • ✓You're between 40 and 75 years old
  • ✓You've been told your cholesterol is high (LDL-C ≥ 100)
  • ✓You've been on a statin for at least 3 months
  • ✓You can make it to our Philadelphia site 3 times
  • ✓You're willing to take an injectable medication

✕Unlikely to qualify

  • ✕You've had a heart attack or stroke in the last 6 months
  • ✕You're pregnant, nursing, or planning pregnancy
  • ✕You have uncontrolled type 1 diabetes
  • ✕You're currently in another clinical trial
  • ✕You have severe liver or kidney disease

Questions you might have

FAQ

Is this safe? The study drug sounds new.

CRP-247 is a Phase III trial — meaning the medication has already passed two earlier rounds of safety testing in hundreds of patients. Phase III compares the drug to placebo to confirm benefit. Every visit includes a safety check. You can withdraw at any time, no questions asked.
