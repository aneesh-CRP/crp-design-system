Compliance rules — locked
· Identify as Clinical Research Philadelphia in the first message of any thread.
· Never include health conditions in a first unsolicited text — only after they've shared it via screener or call.
· Every marketing-class message carries an opt-out (Reply STOP). Reminders/logistics to enrolled participants don't.
· Send window 9 AM – 7 PM ET. No weekends for outreach; reminders OK.
· Never promise outcomes; comp is "up to $X." No emoji, no exclamation marks, no link shorteners.
